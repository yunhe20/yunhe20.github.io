Biography

Hi, I am Yun He. I received my master degree in computer science from Fudan University in 2023, supervised by Prof. Yanwei Fu and Prof. Xiangyang Xue. During my master study, I also worked closely with Dr. Danhang Tang and Dr. Yinda Zhang at Google. Prior to that, I obtained my bachelor degree in computer science from Chongqing University in 2020. My primary research interest is empowering 3D vision via machine learning, including 3D geometry processing, 3D recognition and 3D generation. Recently, I also work as a full-time machine learning engineer to improve the recommendation system by advanced machine learning techniques.
